Our Process

Interior Design Process

How We Work

We transform our clients’ design dreams into reality through detailed space planning and bespoke furniture customizations. Every element is crafted to reflect the unique tastes and preferences of each homeowner. Here’s a glimpse of our workflow.

Online Consultation

We are here to assist you with everything related to home interiors — from the smallest design detail to complete home transformations. Whether you’re planning a cozy makeover, optimizing space, or creating your dream home, our team ensures that every step is smooth, creative, and tailored to your lifestyle.

Approximate Quotation

We provide quick and approximate quotations for all your home interior needs, helping you plan your budget with ease and transparency. From furniture and finishes to complete makeovers, you’ll always have a clear idea of costs before starting your project.

In Person Discussion

We create detailed space plans that perfectly balance functionality and aesthetics. Our approach ensures that every corner of your home is thoughtfully designed and utilized, blending beauty with practicality to match your lifestyle and enhance everyday living.

Advance Booking

We create detailed design concepts with mood boards, 3D renders, and material selections, giving you a clear picture of your future space and ensuring every detail reflects your vision.

2D/3D Design

We create detailed space plans that maximize both functionality and aesthetics, ensuring every corner of your home is thoughtfully utilized. With the help of 2D layouts and realistic 3D designs, you can clearly visualize how your space will look and function before execution, giving you confidence in every decision.

Material Presentation

We create design concepts with mood boards, 3D renders, and material presentations, giving you a clear preview of finishes, fabrics, and colors before execution.

Final Quotation

We design space plans that balance beauty and functionality, supported by 2D layouts and 3D visuals to ensure every corner of your home is practical, stylish, and perfectly utilized.

Execution Begins

We craft design concepts with mood boards and material selections, helping you visualize the overall style while choosing finishes, fabrics, and colors that perfectly match your vision.

Project Handover

During project handover, we carry out detailed inspections and quality checks to ensure flawless finishes and full functionality. Your home is delivered ready to move in, just as envisioned.

At Sharma Interiors, we design and renovate homes, offices, and commercial spaces with creativity and precision. From 2D/3D designs to turnkey execution—experience interior design that truly reflects your style.
